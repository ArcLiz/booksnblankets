About Books 'n Blankets

Why Books 'n Blankets?

Thanks to multiple social media platforms, the bookish community is now more tight knit than ever before. As a result of this, there are numerous online book communities, whether it's open book clubs or specific content creators that have their own discord where they interact with their followers. We love all of these! But, we felt there was a need for a "free-for-all" book community, where every single reader is as important as the next.

That's why we created Discord, so that everyone is equally important and everyone has their moment in the spotlight (if they wish).

The Discord Server

On the Discord server you'll find lots of different types of bookish content. We have (amongst other things)

  • Community Events
  • Buddy Reads Oct 1st
  • Templates, Tips and Tricks
  • Member Stages Promoting Authors, Businesses e.t.c.
  • AI Members Generative Engines and conversational bots that liven up conversation
  • General Chat Channels
  • Chats about Books
  • Book Recommendations (All genres!)
  • Book Reviews
...And So much More!

Behind the Scenes

Although our members make sure that the discord is active, friendly and interesting, there are a few things that needs taking care of behind the scenes, and that's what the Books 'n Blankets Helpers are for! We make sure that everything works, incl. website, discord server, social media accounts and community events. You can always contact us if you have any questions about us, the community or anything we say or do.
